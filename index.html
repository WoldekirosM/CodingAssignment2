Homemade Waffle Recipe

A warm and simple breakfast recipe for crispy golden waffles.

About This Recipe

Waffles are a classic breakfast food that can be enjoyed with syrup, fruit, butter, or whipped cream. This recipe is simple and easy to follow, making it a good choice for a homemade breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat the waffle maker.
  2. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, beat the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  5. Pour batter into the waffle maker and cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or your favorite topping.

Serving Ideas

These waffles can be served with maple syrup, strawberries, blueberries, bananas, powdered sugar, or whipped cream. They can also be paired with eggs or fruit for a full breakfast.
